Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • Apple ID authentication token
  • Inventory items you manually enter
  • Chat messages you send to AI assistant
  • Technical information automatically transmitted during app operation, including IP address and in-app language setting

2. How We Use Your Information

The uses of the collected information are:

  • Authenticate your identity via Apple Sign In
  • Store and manage your personal inventory
  • Process AI chat requests via OpenAI API
  • May review usage patterns and user interactions to improve app features and user experience

3. Information Sharing

We share data with:

  • OpenAI LLC: Chat messages processed by OpenAI API
  • Amazon Web Services: Secure data storage
  • Apple Inc: Authentication services

We do not sell your personal data. If we introduce advertising in future versions, we will update this policy and notify users accordingly.

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored on:

  • AWS DynamoDB servers (encrypted at rest)
  • Your device (inventory images stored locally)

Server logs containing IP addresses and technical information are retained for security and debugging purposes for as long as necessary.

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as:

  • Your account remains active
  • Required by law

6. Your Rights

Right to data deletion: You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at clbrks@outlook.com. Please allow up to 30 days for processing.

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request account and data deletion by contacting us

7. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from users under 13. If we become aware that a user under 13 has provided personal information, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.
